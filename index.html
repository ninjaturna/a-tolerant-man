The Novel

A life too full
for one telling

Bill has been many things to many people — a student, a teacher, a soldier, a traveller, a colleague, a mentor. This is his story, told in his own words: candid, funny, and harder than it looks to have lived.

A Tolerant Man is a memoir in progress. The first readers have already weighed in. What you find here is a record of that early wave of enthusiasm — and an invitation to join it.
